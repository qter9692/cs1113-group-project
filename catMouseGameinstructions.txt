In the game catMouseGame.py you control the mouse by clicking the
mouse. This causes the mouse to change the direction its moving to a
new random direction. To Play the game catMouseGame.py, the goal is
for the mouse to keep collecting cheese and to avoid being caught by
the cat, which is moving in a constant direction. The game ends when
the mouse and cat touch.

catMouseGame by Alec Miller, Ziyuan Jin, and Andrew Duran
